The Best Movies According to Manish

My top 3 movies of all time

Baahubali

A child from the Mahishmati kingdom is raised by tribal people and one day learns about his royal heritage, his father's bravery in battle and a mission to overthrow the incumbent ruler. It's a tale of two cousins in the Kingdom of Mahishmati, India. Balla fights his way with cousin Baahubali for the throne.

Chatrapati

It is a Telugu action-drama film that tells the story of Sivaji, a young man who lives in a small coastal village with his mother. Tragedy strikes when a gang of goons kills his mother and separates him from his sister.

Salaar

Set in the fictional dystopian city-state of Khansaar, the film follows the friendship between Deva (Prabhas), a tribesman, and Varadha (Prithviraj), the prince of Khansaar.
